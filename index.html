About Us

At Educhess, we believe chess is more than just a game—it’s a powerful tool to sharpen focus, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills in children. Our mission is to help young minds develop discipline, patience, and strategic thinking through structured and engaging chess lessons for all skill levels.

Why Choose Educhess?

🎓 Train with a FIDE-rated coach for national & international tournaments.
🧠 Chess boosts focus and teaches kids to think ahead.
⚡ Improves memory & problem-solving skills.
🎨 Enhances creativity & logical thinking.
💪 Builds confidence & resilience.
🏆 Compete in monthly tournaments and improve ranking.

Educhess offers structured online & in-person chess programs for all skill levels. Whether your child is just starting or preparing for competitive play, our expert coach will help them master the game and develop essential life skills.
